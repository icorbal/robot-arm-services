## Verification Rules
1. Check spatial relationships between objects based on their positions
2. Use a tolerance of 0.03m (3cm) for position matching
3. "On top of" means the object's Z position is approximately at the other object's Z + the other object's height
4. "Next to" means within 0.1m horizontally with similar Z height
5. "Picked up" means the object's Z is significantly above the table surface (>0.05m above)
6. Consider object sizes when checking spatial relationships
7. The gripper state matters: if a task involves placing, the gripper should be open after placing
